Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free?

Yes, Blazor Mastery is completely free with no in-app purchases or paywalls.

Does it work offline?

Yes. All content is bundled with the app. No internet connection is required.

How is my progress saved?

Progress is saved locally on your device using secure on-device storage. It persists between sessions automatically.

Which iOS version is required?

Blazor Mastery requires iOS 16 or later.
